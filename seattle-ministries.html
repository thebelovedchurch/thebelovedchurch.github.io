LIFE GROUPS is the small group ministry of Church of the Beloved. It exists to be a place where gospel- centered communities can grow and flourish. We want it to be a “room of grace” where you can come as you are, regardless of where you are in your spiritual journey. We want it to be a place where anyone and everyone can come to explore and experience the love of Christ in community. LIFE GROUPS meet bi-weekly from September to May in different host homes. There are five Life Groups throughout the Puget Sound.

Our Connection Team is here to help you connect with our ministry. We are here to serve you and help answer any questions you may have. First time guests: Be sure to also stop by our Connection Desk in the back of the worship area for a free gift!

Beloved Kids is our ministry for children. We've created an exciting and fun environment for our kids to learn about the Bible and Jesus Christ. First, stop by the Check In Station, where a friendly volunteer will be there to welcome you and help you check in your child. You can then drop off your child at the gym. We'll begin with a time of exciting praise and a short teaching on the lesson of the week. We then separate into four classes, where they continue to learn through various activies, crafts and games.

The four classes are:

18 months to 2 years old

3 to 5 years old

K to 2nd graders

3rd to 5th graders
